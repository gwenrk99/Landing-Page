Barbie

Summary

The Barbie movie follows stereotypical Barbie and Ken in their world until Barbie isn't "perfect" anymore and begins malfunctioning. They set off on the journey to the real world to set things right. Along the way, Barbie and Ken have their own realizations of their identities and their realities.

Author: Gwendolyn Kramarczyk, 2023
